Descriptions
 
A dataset containing containing a sighting indicator for each individual during each boat surveys.
 
Format:
 
  A dataframe with 32315 rows and 7 variables:
 
  ID: Dolphin "name"
  
  Mark level: Goodness of marking of the individuals, one of well marked ("WM"), fairly marked ("FM") and unmarked ("UM")
       
  Age class: Age class of the individuals
  
  Gender: Gender of the individuals, one of "F", "M", "U"
  
  TOT: Total number of sighting days
  
  Data: Date of sighting
  
  Observed: 1 if during the research day, the individual was sighted 
            0 otherwise
